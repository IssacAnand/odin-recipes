Mac and Cheese

Description

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter and flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Breadcrumbs

Boil the noodles, drain them, and transfer them to a greased baking dish. Prepare the cheese sauce, then pour it over the noodles, stirring to coat them evenly. Make the topping and spread it over the macaroni and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika for color and flavor. Bake the macaroni and cheese until the topping is golden brown and crispy.
