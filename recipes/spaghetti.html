Spaghetti

Description

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Beef
  • Butter
  • Cottage Cheese, Cream Cheese and Sour Cream
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese

To prepare the dish, first boil spaghetti in salted walter until al dente, then drain and set it aside. COok ground beef on the stove, drain off grease and, transfer the beef to bowl, mixing the sauce thoroughly. Prepare baking dish and spread half of the spaghetti on the bottom. Combine cottage and Cream cheese and sour cream in bowl, layer this mixture into spaghetti. Add remaining spaghetti on top with butter. Pour beef and sauce mixture over spaghetti, spreading it evenly. Bake the dish in oven for 30 minutes. Then sprinkle cheese on top and continue baking until cheese melted and bubbly.
